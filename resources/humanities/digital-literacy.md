## Overview

Digital literacy is the ability to access, evaluate, create, and share information using digital tools. Community digital literacy programs help people participate in education, public services, employment, and civic life.

## Core Skills

| Skill | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| Device basics | Enables confident use of phones, tablets, and computers |
| Search skills | Helps users find relevant information |
| Source evaluation | Reduces misinformation and fraud risk |
| Online safety | Protects privacy, accounts, and personal data |
| Digital communication | Supports school, work, and public participation |

## Program Design Tips

Start with real community needs, teach through practical tasks, provide accessible language support, and leave time for guided practice.
